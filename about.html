Who we are

Work the Numbers is a Western Australian based business with the aim of being Perth's best and most trusted business improvement service. At Work the Numbers, we aim to provide small businesses with the tools and know how to grow their profit margins.

For our Hospitality associates we can provide the following:

  • Analysis of Profit and Loss statements to identify trends and areas of opportunity for improved profit
  • Education on food cost systems and stocktakes
  • Providing information and guides on food cost improvement methods
  • Training on rostering and labour control practices
  • Performing stocktakes
  • Customer service workshops

For the construction sector, Work the Numbers can provide the following services:

  • Preparation of budget estimates and Bill of Quantities
  • Preparation of tender documentation
  • Assistance in the design of new homes and renovations
  • Referrals to trades to help complete the project

We here at Work the Numbers value your privacy and understand that your business has worked hard to establish prices from your suppliers, and can guarantee that all tasks will be handled with discretion. Your information will not be shared with anyone and will be handled with the strictest confidence.
